Wherever you look ... Inviting Elegance

Standing tall with two 28-story high-rise towers, home to 640 units, including studios, and one and two-bedroom units. 3600 was built in 1960 and is located in the Lakeview East neighborhood on Addison and Lake Shore Drive. 3600 is steps from the Lincoln Park lakefront path, Broadway, Halsted and Southport shopping and dining, the Chicago Cubs at Wrigley Field, and public transportation to downtown Chicago. Amenities include: 24 hour door staff, fitness and weight room, commissary, laundry, rooftop deck, an award-winning patio with outdoor seating and grills, and valet parking.

24/7 Concierge

24/7 door staff are available to address your questions and warmly welcome your guests.

Valet Parking

Valet parking is available 24/7. Pioneer Parking is the managing agent. Please see or call the garage manager for parking registration, car wash services, purchase of guest parking coupons or to sign up for the self park wait list. 773-296-0286

Patio

The award winning patio has ample outdoor seating and grills for seasonal use.

Sundeck

Enjoy spectacular lake and city views from atop the two sundecks located on the 29th floor of each tower.

Fitness

The fitness center has cardio equipment and free weights and is available to residents 24 hours/7 days.

Commissary

The Commissary is a convenient stop to pick up deliveries and essentials including food, beverages and snacks.

Laundry

Commercial washers and dryers are available in both towers.

Controlled Access

Access to all common area entrances require a key fob that can be obtained from the Management office.
